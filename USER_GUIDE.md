# 📖 User Guide: Mental Health Day Booking System

Welcome! This guide will walk you through how to use the Mental Health Day Booking System to book your day off.

## 🎯 What You Can Do

This system allows you to:
- Book a mental health day from school
- See which dates are available
- Receive confirmation of your booking
- Include any additional information with your request

## 🚀 How to Book a Day

### Step 1: Access the Application
Open the website in your web browser. You should see the booking page with a calendar.

### Step 2: Select Your Date
1. Look at the calendar display
2. **Available dates** appear in normal colors
3. **Unavailable dates** (already booked, weekends, holidays) appear grayed out or disabled
4. Click on your preferred date

### Step 3: Fill in Your Information
After selecting a date, you'll see a form with the following fields:

#### Required Fields (must fill in):
- **📅 Date** - The date you selected (shows automatically)
- **👤 Name** - Your full name
- **✉️ Email** - Your email address

#### Optional Field:
- **📝 Additional Information** - You can add a note here if you need to explain your request (e.g., "I need a mental health break," "Doctor's appointment," etc.)

### Step 4: Submit Your Booking
1. Review your information
2. Click the **"Submit Booking"** or **"Confirm"** button
3. You should see a confirmation message

### Step 5: Check Your Email
- You'll receive an email confirmation at the address you provided
- This confirms your booking has been recorded
- Keep this email for your records

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I book multiple days?
Yes! You can book as many mental health days as allowed. However, check with your school's policy on limits.

### Can I see which dates are already booked?
Yes! Booked dates will appear differently on the calendar (usually grayed out or marked as unavailable).

### What if I need to cancel my booking?
Contact your school administrator. You may need to provide your name, email, or booking date.

### What dates can I NOT book?
You cannot book:
- Weekends (Saturdays & Sundays)
- School holidays or scheduled breaks
- Dates already booked by the system as unavailable
- Past dates (dates that have already passed)

If a date is unavailable, it will be disabled on the calendar.

### What if I made a mistake in my booking?
Contact your school administrator with:
- Your name
- Your email address
- The date of your booking
- What needs to be corrected

They can update or delete your booking.

### Will my information be kept private?
Yes! Your information is:
- Stored securely on the school's server
- Only accessible to school administrators
- Never shared with anyone outside the school
- Used only for tracking mental health day bookings

### How will the school know about my booking?
Your booking will appear in the admin panel, which your school staff can review. The administrator will have a list of all bookings by date and student name.

### Can I see my booking history?
Contact your school administrator if you need to check your past bookings.

### What if the website doesn't work?
Try these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Refresh the page**
   - Press `F5` or `Ctrl+R` (Windows) / `Cmd+R` (Mac)
   
2. **Clear your browser cache**
   - Close the browser completely and reopen it
   
3. **Try a different browser**
   - If it works in another browser, your original browser may have issues
   
4. **Check internet connection**
   - Make sure you're connected to the internet
   
5. **Contact your administrator**
   - If the issue persists, let your school administrator know

### What information should I include in "Additional Information"?
You can include:
- A brief reason for your request
- Any relevant details (e.g., "Has appointment," "Feeling overwhelmed," etc.)
- Any specific notes for the administrator

Keep it professional and brief.

### Can I book for someone else?
No, you should only book for yourself. If someone needs to book for you, they should contact the school administrator.

## 📱 Using on Different Devices

### Desktop / Laptop
- Click on dates in the calendar
- Use the form fields to enter information
- Best experience for detailed viewing

### Mobile / Tablet
- Tap on dates in the calendar
- The form is designed to work on smaller screens
- Scroll down to see all form fields

## 🔒 Privacy and Security Notes

- **Don't share your email password** - The system only needs your email address for confirmation
- **Use a valid email** - Make sure you use an email you can access
- **Check confirmation emails** - Keep copies of your confirmation emails
